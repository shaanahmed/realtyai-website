Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026

1. Privacy approach

RealtyAI is built with a local-first workflow. Listing work is intended to stay on your Windows device except where you choose to use third-party services.

2. Information we collect

  • Support details you submit by email or support portal
  • Basic website usage data from standard hosting logs
  • Purchase/licensing details handled by Microsoft Store systems

3. Information not intentionally collected by this website

  • No website account signup data
  • No payment card processing on this website
  • No hidden analytics profile tracking by default

4. How app data is handled

  • Listing records are designed for local desktop workflow
  • Cloud AI requests are sent only to providers you configure
  • API keys are stored in encrypted form within the app environment

5. Third-party services

  • Microsoft Store for purchase, licensing, and distribution
  • Zoho Desk for support ticketing and knowledge base
  • Optional cloud AI providers selected by the user

Each third-party platform has its own policies and terms.

6. User responsibilities

  • Protect access to your computer and local files
  • Keep API credentials and account credentials private
  • Review generated output before business use

7. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to support@realtyaihq.com.

Policy updates

This page may be updated when product, legal, or platform requirements change. The latest effective date appears at the top of this page.
